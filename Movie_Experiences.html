D-BOX

Discover D-Box

Discover a hyper-realistic, immersive entertainment experience that moves the body and sparks the imagination through motion.

Motion Experience. Get the best seat in the house with captivating motion.
D-Box seats vibrate and move along with the events on-screen, adding an extra dimension to the movie.

**WARNING**

The D-Box motion system may be harmful to the eldery, pregnant women, people with conditions including heart, back, head, or neck, injuries or pre-existing medical conditions. Use of D-Box is not recommended for children under ten years of age without parental supervision. Min. weight must be atleast 45lbs/20.41kg. Any weight lighter may not activate the seat. Use of hot liquids in the surrounding area of the D-Box System must be avoided at all times to prevent spillage which could cause serious injuries to the user.

  • Starlux Brampton Cinemas
